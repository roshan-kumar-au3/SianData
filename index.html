CEMENT QUALITY PREDICTION SYSTEM

To forecast cement quality in real-time at any point in production process. Customer wanted to replace the empirical models with automated prediction system.

HOT STRIP ROLLING ANOMALY DETECTION

Detection of any defect during hot strip rolling procedure and assuring the completion of process without any hindrance. Automating the coil monitoring systems and reducing the testing time. Hence, giving field experts more time to apply their expertise into production.

Predict the Mechanical, and Physical. Properties. Yield Stress, Ultimate Tensile Strength, Elongation of Bars/Re-Bars.The solution can assist the operators in field to infer the physical properties significantly reducing the testing time.

Predicting the delay in batch prior to start of rolling process. Predicting the yield estimates using the same batch data. Identify the delays at specific points on rolling mill during the rolling process. Providing a preventive maintenance system
